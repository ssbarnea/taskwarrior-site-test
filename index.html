The Taskwarrior distribution contains the essential core components. The open source community provides many non-core components, distributed separately, because they are not tied to Taskwarrior release schedule.

Projects are sorted by most recent release date. Do you have a taskwarrior extension you'd like to see listed here? Do you have any corrections to suggest? Please tell us about it.

Some of the projects are now obsolete, or suspected obsolete, and have been relocated.
